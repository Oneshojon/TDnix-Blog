About Me

This is what I do.
Hi, I'm Titus Denis

Former process engineer in oil & gas → now becoming a full-stack Python developer.

After two years in the refinery industry and standing up for fair working conditions, I chose to pivot into tech — a field where I can create tools that solve real problems and make an immediate impact.

I'm currently deep into Angela Yu's 100 Days of Python Bootcamp (Day 72+) and have built projects ranging from automated gym booking systems and stock alerts to full-stack web applications using Flask, SQLAlchemy, Flask-Login, and Bootstrap.

I’ve also taught basic programming to children as a coding instructor, which honed my ability to break down complex concepts into simple, understandable steps.

This blog is my space to document the journey, share projects, and explore how engineering thinking translates into software development. My goal for 2026: ship valuable tools, secure meaningful remote or freelance work, and inspire others to make bold career changes.
